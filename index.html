Kenan Jiang

Kenan is currently pursuing his Master's degree in Computer Science at Emory University.
He graduated from UC Berkeley with a Bachelor degree in Computer Science and Applied Math in 2021 and subsequently worked as an ML Software Engineer at Linqia for 2.5 years.
He interned at ERIC Lab at UC Santa Cruz from 2022 to 2024.
Kenan's research interests span NLP, Vision-Language Models, and Digital Humanities.
Outside of academia, Kenan enjoys practicing Wing Chun and playing his Erhu.
